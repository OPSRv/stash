01 · Philosophy

A deliberate «Frankenstein»
instead of a cluttered dock.

A real day isn’t one fullscreen app — it’s a dozen tiny actions per minute: translate, copy, grab a video, start a timer. Stash gives them one address.

I.

UI / UX

Predictable interactions, instant feedback, proper focus management, dark and light themes, reduced-motion, a11y from line one.

II.

Modularity

Every module is standalone — its own api.ts, its own tests, a lazy popup. No cross-module imports.

III.

Performance

Lazy tabs, prefetch on hover, nothing heavy in the popup-open path. The popup should appear faster than you can blink.
